HealthEPetz is the Pet Owner’s Portal to help manage their pet’s health and healthcare costs
It’s difficult enough to afford your own medical costs, let along your pets

HealthEPetz provides tools to help optimize your pet’s health and lower costs for care including:

  • Track your pet’s medical and vaccination history
  • Vaccination and other important pet care reminders
  • Find a licensed vet
  • Compare prices for vet services
  • Chat with a virtual vet
  • Schedule a televet or home vet visit appointment
  • Get latest pet health news and tips
  • Get information on coupons and specials from local and online pet supply stores
